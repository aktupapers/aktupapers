Who We Are

AKTU Papers (aktupapers.com) is a student-driven initiative dedicated to helping Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow students excel in their exams. We provide free, easy-to-access previous year question papers (PYQs) for all semesters, branches, and subjects.

What started as a small collection of shared PDFs has now grown into a comprehensive digital library trusted by thousands of AKTU students every semester.

🎯 Our Mission

To remove the barrier of finding quality exam material. Every AKTU student, whether from CS, EC, ME, CE, or any other branch, should get free and organised previous year papers without any login or payment. We believe exam preparation becomes 10x easier when you have the right resources.

📄 Semester-wise Papers

⚡ Free & No Signup
