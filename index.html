About AKTU Previous Year Papers

AKTUPapers.com is a free resource for students of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), formerly known as UPTU. We provide previous year question papers for all courses including B.Tech, MBA, MCA, B.Pharma, and M.Tech — covering all semesters from 2018 to 2025.

Why Use AKTU Papers for Exam Prep?

Solving AKTU previous year question papers helps you understand the exam pattern, important topics, and marking scheme. Regular practice with papers from 2020–2024 gives you the best chance to score high in your semester exams. All papers are official AKTU end-semester and mid-semester question papers.

All Branches & Courses Covered

We cover all major AKTU branches: Computer Science (CS), Information Technology (IT), Mechanical (ME), Electronics (EC), Electrical (EE), Civil (CE), AI & ML, and Chemical (CH) for B.Tech. For PG: MBA, MCA, M.Tech, and M.Pharma. All 8 semesters included.
